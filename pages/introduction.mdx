# Welcome to Scrollery

Scrollery is a specialized UI library that empowers developers to effortlessly animate contents as users scroll through websites/web applications. Our vision is to provide a comprehensive set of tools and components specifically designed to enhance the visual experience of websites/web applications and bring them to life with captivating scroll animations. With Scrollery, you can create stunning, interactive, and engaging image animations that leave a lasting impression on your users.

## Key Features

- <strong className="text-[#0DBBA5]">Visual Experience Enhancement</strong>:
  Whether you're building a portfolio website, a creative landing page, or an
  interactive storytelling platform, Scrollery empowers you to effortlessly
  bring your designs to life with captivating scroll animations.

- <strong className="text-[#0DBBA5]">Simple Integration</strong>: We understand
  the importance of simplicity and efficiency in the development process.
  Scrollery offers a user-friendly and straightforward integration process,
  allowing you to easily animate contents with minimal setup. Our library
  provides a set of intuitive components that seamlessly work with your existing
  codebase, enabling you to focus on creating visually impactful scroll
  animations without unnecessary complexity.

- <strong className="text-[#0DBBA5]">Customization and Flexibility</strong>:
  Every image has a unique story to tell, and we want to help you showcase it in
  the most captivating way possible. Scrollery provides a wide range of
  customization options, empowering you to tailor the image animations to suit
  your project's specific requirements. Adjust animation timings, effects,
  transitions, and more to create personalized and immersive experiences that
  perfectly align with your design vision.
